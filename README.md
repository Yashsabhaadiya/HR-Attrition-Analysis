# 👥 HR Attrition Analysis Project

## 📌 Project Overview
Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges 
companies face today. This project analyzes the IBM HR 
Analytics dataset to understand **why employees leave 
the company** and what factors contribute the most to 
attrition. The goal is to help HR teams make better 
decisions to retain their employees.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
> **Why are employees leaving the company and what 
can be done to reduce attrition?**

## 💡 Key Insights
1. 📍 Overall attrition rate is **16.1%**
2. 📍 **Sales department** has the highest attrition rate
3. 📍 Employees doing **OverTime** are more likely to leave
4. 📍 Employees with **low monthly income** leave more often
5. 📍 **Single employees** have higher attrition than married
6. 📍 **Younger employees** (25-35) leave more than older ones
7. 📍 Employees with **low job satisfaction** are more likely to leave
8. 📍 Employees with **0-2 years** at company have highest attrition

---

## ✅ Conclusions & Recommendations
- 💰 **Increase salaries** for low income employees to reduce attrition
- ⏰ **Reduce overtime** to improve work life balance
- 😊 **Improve job satisfaction** through better work environment
- 🎯 **Focus on Sales department** as it has highest attrition
- 👶 **Support younger employees** with mentorship and growth opportunities
